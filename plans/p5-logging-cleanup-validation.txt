Implementation notes:

  `slog` levels and keep the existing in-memory ring buffer behavior.
- Supported Unix platforms use standard-library `log/syslog`; unsupported
  platforms and syslog failures fall back to stderr.
- Repository search found no internal callers of `InitLogger` or `GetLogger`.
  Migration note for external Go consumers was added to the plan and changelogs.
